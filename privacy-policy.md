# Spicci Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2026**
**Version: 1**

## 1. Overview

Spicci is a personal finance application built for individual use. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data.

## 2. Data We Collect

Spicci collects the following data to provide its core functionality:

- Financial account information (account names, types, balances) obtained through Plaid
- Transaction history (dates, amounts, payees, categories) obtained through Plaid
- Categories, recurring bills, and budget data you enter manually
- App configuration settings (backend URL, notification preferences)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary to display your financial data within the app.

## 3. How Your Data Is Stored

All financial data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database. Plaid access tokens — the credentials used to retrieve your financial data — are stored using Android Keystore-backed AES-256-GCM encryption and never leave your device.

We do not operate servers that store your financial data. The Firebase backend used by this app acts as a pass-through to the Plaid API and does not retain your data.

## 4. Plaid

This app uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. to connect to your financial institutions. By linking an account, you agree to Plaid's Privacy Policy available at [https://plaid.com/legal/privacy-policy](https://plaid.com/legal/privacy-policy). Plaid's use of your data is governed by their policy, not this one.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your financial data with any third parties. Your data is not used for advertising or analytics purposes.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained on your device for as long as you use the app. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data. You can also remove individual accounts and their associated transactions within the app.

## 7. Security

We implement the following security measures:

- Biometric authentication (fingerprint or PIN) required on app launch
- AES-256-GCM encryption for Plaid access tokens via Android Keystore
- TLS 1.2+ for all network communications
- Android OS-level disk encryption for stored data

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access all data stored by the app (viewable within the app)
- Delete your data (by removing accounts or uninstalling the app)
- Opt out of optional features such as notifications at any time

## 9. Children

This app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, you will be prompted to review and accept the updated version before continuing to use the app.

## 11. Contact

This is a personal-use application. For questions about this privacy policy, contact the app developer directly.
